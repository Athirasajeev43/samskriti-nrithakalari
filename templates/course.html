Bharatanatyam

Description: Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest classical dance forms of India originating from Tamil Nadu. It is known for its graceful hand gestures (mudras), expressive facial expressions, and precise footwork. This dance form combines rhythm, emotion, and storytelling based on Indian mythology and traditional themes.

Students will learn basic adavus (steps), body posture, hand gestures, facial expressions, and simple dance sequences.
